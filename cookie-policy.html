Cookie Policy

This page explains which cookies or similar technologies Hablawithflow uses, and how visitors can control optional analytics or marketing cookies.

Last updated: 28 March 2026 Controller: Hablawithflow Contact: hello@hablawithflow.com

How Cookies Are Used

Cookies and similar technologies can store or access information on your device. Under EU rules, non-essential cookies should not be used unless you have first been given clear information and a real choice.

Necessary Cookies

Necessary cookies are used for core functions such as login continuity, security, booking flow continuity, and saving cookie preferences themselves. These remain active because the site cannot operate properly without them.

Analytics Cookies

Analytics cookies are optional. They should only be activated after you opt in. They may be used to measure page performance, navigation patterns, or service improvement.

Marketing Cookies

Marketing cookies are optional. They should only be activated after you opt in. They may be used for campaign measurement, audience building, remarketing, or ad platform integrations if Hablawithflow enables them in future.

Your Choices

  • You can accept all, reject optional cookies, or manage analytics and marketing separately.
  • You can reopen cookie settings at any time from the footer button on the site.
  • Withdrawing consent is designed to be as easy as giving it.

Current Site Position

Hablawithflow keeps optional analytics and marketing cookie categories off by default until you opt in. Optional marketing emails are also separate from cookie preferences and require their own checkbox.
